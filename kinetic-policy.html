Privacy Policy for Kinetic Sand

Effective Date: October 8, 2025

Thank you for playing Kinetic Sand. This privacy policy describes how we handle information in our mobile application.

Data Collection and Usage

Kinetic Sand is a simple, offline game. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

  • Accelerometer Data: The game uses your device's accelerometer for gameplay control only. This data is processed in real-time on your device and is never stored, collected, or transmitted off the device.
  • Local Storage: The app uses local storage (SharedPreferences) to save your top 3 high scores and the corresponding "Sand Art" images. This data is stored only on your own device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Kinetic Sand does not use any third-party services that collect information from you. There are no ads, analytics, or external libraries that track user data.
